Salty Goat Grill

Family-Owned Diner, Southern Staples, Small Mom & Pop Environment

Welcome to Salty Goat Grill, a cozy family-owned diner that brings you the heart and soul of Southern cuisine in a small mom and pop environment. Step into a warm, nostalgic atmosphere where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and homemade biscuits fills the air. Our menu features Southern staples like crispy fried chicken, fluffy buttermilk pancakes, savory biscuits and gravy, and mouthwatering apple pie. Every dish is made with love and tradition. Come join us for a taste of Southern comfort and hospitality that will make you feel right at home.
